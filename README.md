# SUPERMARKET SALES DATA ANALYSIS

## Project Objective
The supermarket wants to create a sales report from three different stores in three cities to know about the sales and to implement more ideas to grow sales.

## To Resolve these Questions
- Which city has highest sales?
- Which product has the highest sales?
- Who purchased more -  Men or Women?
- To know the preferred payment type
- To identify trend over time.
- Relationship between product unit price and category.

## Project Insights
- Naypyitaw is the top-performing and highest-margin city, representing the strongest business opportunity.
- Sports & Travel and Home & Lifestyle lead sales, while Health & Beauty underperforms.
- Cash payments dominate, reflecting traditional buying behavior.
- Customer demographics are evenly split, but female members represent the largest single segment.
- Sales patterns show predictable demand spikes, useful for forecasting and operational planning.

## Conclusion
The dashboard insights indicate that Naypyitaw offers the highest revenue and margin potential, making it the ideal target for expanded marketing and inventory investment. Strengthening top-performing categories like Sports & Travel and Home & Lifestyle, while introducing promotions for underperforming segments such as Health & Beauty, can further optimize sales. Additionally, encouraging digital payment adoption through incentives may enhance customer experience and streamline transactions.
